Privacy Policy — Pyramid Festival App

Last updated: July 2, 2026

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Pyramid Festival ("we", "us") provides the Pyramid Festival mobile app (the "App"). This policy explains what the App does and does not do with your information.

What data the App handles

  • Your saved plan and reminders. When you star an event, that choice and its reminder are stored only on your device. It is never uploaded to us or anyone else, and it is removed when you unstar the event or delete the App.
  • Local notifications. Reminders are scheduled and delivered on your device by the operating system. We do not operate a push-notification server and receive no information about the notifications you get.
  • Festival content. The App downloads the public festival schedule, map, and info from our servers. These are ordinary read-only requests for public content and include no personal information about you.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not ask you to create an account or sign in.
  • We do not collect your name, email, contacts, photos, or location.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share any data, because we do not collect any.

Permissions

  • Notifications. Requested only so the App can remind you about events you saved. You can grant or deny this at any time in your device settings; the rest of the App works normally without it.

Children

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the new version at this URL.
